Empowering Women




Mission Statement:


To empower women with support and resources to find their way through childhood/adult trauma.

Who we are:



    We volunteer time and/or offer a sliding fee scale


  • We've experienced trauma and have continued our education in social services/mental health

  • We know that financial barriers prevent many woman from getting the help they want and need

What we offer:



    One on one support

  • Talk therapy/counseling

    • Group support

  • Our groups explore the impact of trauma, identifying negative and positive ways it has impacted our lives, validating our feelings

  • Meditation

    • Computers

  • Access to positive programs and resources for women

    • A library

  • Books about the mind, trauma, self help and other resources available
